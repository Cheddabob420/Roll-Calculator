How to Use

  1. Enter Total Run Length: Input the total footage of your run.
  2. Subtract Roll Footages: Each time you click "Next", enter the footage of the roll you're using and the system will subtract it.
  3. Monitor Remaining Footage: The display shows your remaining footage. It turns red when below 8000 ft.
  4. View Results: When you go negative, you'll see the final butt roll footage and whether it's suitable to return to storage.

Color Indicators

  • Green - Butt roll is 8000 ft or more (good to return)
  • Red - Butt roll is under 8000 ft (too small, consider splitting another roll)
